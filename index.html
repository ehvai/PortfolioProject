I was born in Perth, Australia. Sadly, I don't have an accent at all. I have spent most of my life in the United States on the East Coast.

Ever since the internet has been a thing, web pages have become more than just a list of links or words. They have expanded to be a visual representation of an idea. Devleoping a webpage is a way to showcase our creativity while allowing the logical side to get to work.

Working alone on a project is great. It feels good to get it done and know that you put in all the work for it. What is even better, though, is working with a group of people and having that sense of accomplishment when it is done that you can share with your teammates.

I am a gamer at heart. I get to spend time with friends online exploring new worlds. My love of gaming is not exlusive to video games, I also enjoy playing D&D in all its various incarnations with the same group of friends I have known for 20 years. The best part about it is creating memories that we talk about years later.

I also enjoy crafting including painting, drawing and anything that lets me work with my hands. Although I don't do it often enough, crafting lets me explore other creative outlets. My sister is an avid crafter and we go to gaming conventions where we make interesting things for very interesting people.
